Reflex: reflexive

Boolean feature. Is this pronoun or verb reflexive? Many tagsets would have this as another value of “prontype”. As far as pronouns are concerned, we could probably live with it since we made “poss” a separate feature and I only know about two different reflexive classes: personal and possessive. On the other hand, verbs can be also tagged as reflexive and then I would turn the feature on for them. (But the examples I know about are in fact fused verbs with reflexive pronouns, as in Spanish “despertarse” or Russian “проснуться” = “wake up”. We will need a general approach to fused words of different parts of speech but I don’t think we’ve got any at the moment, do we?)

Yes: it is reflexive

Note that there is no “no” value. If the word is not possessive, the feature will just not be mentioned in the FEAT column. (Which means that empty value has the “no” meaning.)

Examples: [cs] se, si, svůj
