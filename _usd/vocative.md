## vocative: vocative

The *vocative* relation is used to mark dialogue participant addressed in text (common in emails and newsgroup postings). The relation links the addressee's name to its host sentence.

<div class="sd-parse">
Guys , take it easy!
vocative(take, Guys)
</div>

<div class="sd-parse">
Marie , comment vas - tu ?
vocative(vas, Marie)
</div>
