## neg: negation modifier

The negation modifier is the relation between a negation word and the
word it modifies.

<div class="sd-parse">
Bill is not a scientist
neg(scientist, not)
</div>

<div class="sd-parse">
Bill does n't drive
neg(drive, n't)
</div>
