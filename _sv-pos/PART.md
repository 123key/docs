The `PART` is only currently used in Swedish for the infinitive marker _att_. Note that not all instances of _att_ are of type `PART`, as _att_ can also be used as a [SCONJ]().

### Example

- _det är möjligt <b>att</b> förändra den_ "it is possible <b>to</b> change it"
