## `{{ page.title }}`: {{ page.shortdef }}

The possessive modifier relation is a subtype of the [case]()
relation. It holds between a noun and the genitive *'s* modifying it.

~~~ sdparse
Bill 's clothes
case:poss(Bill-1, 's-2)
nmod(clothes-3, Bill-1)
~~~
