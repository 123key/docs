## poss : possession modifier
The possession modifier relation holds between the head of an NP and its possessive determiner, or a genitive 's complement. 

~~~ sdparse
their offices
poss(offices, their)
~~~



~~~ sdparse
Bill 's clothes
poss(clothes, Bill)
~~~
