## poss : possessive modifier
The possessive modifier relation is a subtype of the *case* relation. It holds between a noun and the genitive 's modifying it. 

~~~ sdparse
Bill 's clothes
possessive(Bill-1, 's-2)
nmod(clothes-3, Bill-1)
~~~
