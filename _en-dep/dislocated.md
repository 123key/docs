title:  'dislocated'
shortdef : 'dislocated elements'
---

The `dislocated` relation is used for fronted or postposed elements
that do not fulfill the usual core grammatical relations of a
sentence.

~~~ sdparse
This is our office, me and Sam.
dislocated(our, me)
cc(me, and)
conj(me, Sam)
~~~
