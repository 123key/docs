Aux chain

Auxiliary dependencies should not form a chain.

Search expression: _ <aux (_ <aux _)

Positive (correct) example:


Reagan has died
aux(died-3, has-2)

Negative (incorrect) example:


Do you think that he will have left when we come ?
aux(think, Do)
aux(left, will)
aux(left, have)
