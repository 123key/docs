Introduction

The Japanese corpora annotated according to the UD annotation scheme will be obtained by conversion from several resources.

As a first step, we construct conversion rules on ‘NTT Japanese phrase structure treebank’ (Tanaka and Nagata 2013) on Mainichi Newspaper. We also try to construct conversion rules on ‘Balanced Corpus of Contemporary Written Japanese’(BCCWJ) (Maekawa et al. 2014) with third party annotations (Mori et al. 2014).
