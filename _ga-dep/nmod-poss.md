shortdef : 'possessive pronoun'

Irish denotes possession through the use of possessive pronouns: 

 * _mo_ "my"
 * _do_ "your" (SG),
 * _a_  "his/her"
 * _ár_ "our"
 * _bhur_ "your" (PL),
 * _a_ "their"

~~~ sdparse
Chuir mé ceist ar mo mhúinteoir \n I asked my teacher a question
nmod:poss(mhúinteoir, mo)
~~~
