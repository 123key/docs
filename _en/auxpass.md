## auxpass : passive auxiliary

A passive auxiliary of a clause is a non-main verb of the clause which
contains the passive information.

<div class="sd-parse">
Kennedy has been killed
auxpass(killed, been)
</div>

<div class="sd-parse">
Kennedy was\/got killed
auxpass(killed, was/got)
</div>
