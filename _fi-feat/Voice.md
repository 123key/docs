The `Voice` feature captures the distinction between active
and passive for [verbs](fi-pos/VERB).

### `Act`: active voice

Examples:

* [fi] _**valita** paikka_ “_**select** a place_”

### `Pass`: passive voice

Examples:

* [fi] _paikka on **valittu**_ “_a place has been **selected**_”
