## num : numeric modifier

A numeric modifier of a noun is any number phrase that serves to
modify the meaning of the noun with a quantity.

<div class="sd-parse">
Sam ate 3 sheep
num(sheep, 3)
</div>

<div class="sd-parse">
Sam spent forty dollars
num(dollars, forty)
</div>

<div class="sd-parse">
Sam spent $ 40
num($, 40)
</div>
