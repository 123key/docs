## aux : auxiliary

An auxiliary of a clause is a non-main verb of the clause, e.g., a
modal auxiliary, or a form of "be", "do" or "have" in a periphrastic
tense.

<div class="sd-parse">
Reagan has died
aux(died, has)
</div>

<div class="sd-parse">
He should leave
aux(leave, should)
</div>
