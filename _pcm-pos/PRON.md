### Definition

Pronouns are words that substitute for [nouns](NOUN) or noun phrases, whose meaning is recoverable from the linguistic or extralinguistic
context.

Possessive pronouns and personal pronoun are annotated as pronouns.


### The pronouns of Naija include :

- I (first-person, singular, personal pronoun, subject) :
  - <b>I</b> want drive.
- me (first-person, singular, personal pronoun, complement) :
  - Follow <b>me</b>.
- ma (first-person, singular, possessive pronoun) :
  - I go look for one of <b>ma</b> boy.
  
- you (second-person, singular, personal pronoun, subject/complement)
  - <b>You</b> dey drive.
  - e fit sharply carry <b>you</b>.
- your (second-person, singular, possessive pronoun)
  - <b>Your</b> tyre dey very important.

- im (third-person, singular)
  - possessive determiner : <b>Im</b> daughter fall from three storey build for school.
  - personal pronoun , subject : <b>Im</b> pick di call.

- una (generic pronoun)
  - Make <b>una</b> try.
